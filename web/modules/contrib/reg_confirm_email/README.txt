INTRODUCTION
------------

This modules extracts LoginToboggan's email confirmation feature.
It adds a confirm email address field under email address field in
the registration form.

If you want your users to rewrite their email address during registration
but don't want to install the whole LoginTobogganmodule, 
then this module is for you.

INSTALLATION
------------

1. Download, install and enable the module
2. Go to "admin/config/people/accounts"
3. Enable confirm email by ticking the "Use two e-mail fields on registration 
   form" under "Confirm email address"
